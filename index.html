Character Review: Luna Lovegood

Luna Lovegood is a Harry Potter Character. She appears in book 5 when Ron and Hermione had to sit in the prefect compartment Harry finds himself sitting with Ginny, Neville and Luna. Luna is ecentric she was told of creature called crumple horned snorkaks or Nargles etc. Even in the wizarding world those creatures were not real.
